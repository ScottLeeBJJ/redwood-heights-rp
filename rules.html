Redwood Heights Server Rules

1. Respect All Players ▶

1. Respect is a priority.

*Players must treat everyone fairly, regardless of gender, race, or nationality.

  • Redwood Heights is a roleplaying community where respect is a priority. Players must treat everyone fairly, regardless of gender, race, or nationality.
  • No harassment: Avoid bullying, repeated unwanted interactions, or verbal attacks.
  • No hate speech: Discriminatory language based on race, gender, or identity is not tolerated.
  • No toxic behavior: Deliberate aggravation or provocation outside roleplay is unacceptable.

2. Roleplay at All Times (In-Character Behavior)

*Players must stay in character at all times to maintain immersion.

  • OOC Chats: Use out-of-character chat sparingly and mark it with (( )) or use designated OOC channels.
  • Immersion: Avoid references to real-world events or breaking character in-game unless absolutely necessary.

3. No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

*Attacking or killing another player without an in-character reason is prohibited.

  • Conflict escalation: Ensure proper buildup before any violent actions, such as arguments or ongoing tensions.
  • Revenge killing: Do not kill someone for revenge without a justified in-character reason.

4. No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

*Running over or using vehicles to kill players without roleplay justification is not allowed.

  • Accidental VDM: If you accidentally hit another player, roleplay the situation realistically (e.g., call emergency services).

5. No Metagaming!

*Do not use out-of-character information to influence in-game decisions.

  • Knowledge separation: Keep in-game knowledge and out-of-character information separate.
  • OOC voice chats: Don’t use OOC voice chats to relay in-game information.

6. No Powergaming

*Powergaming involves forcing actions onto other players without allowing them to respond.

  • Give others a chance: Allow players to respond to your actions instead of forcing outcomes (e.g., instead of “I knock you out,” say “I attempt to knock you out, what do you do?”).
  • Avoid unrealistic actions: Don’t perform actions that your character wouldn’t be able to do based on their abilities.

7. No Exploiting or Hacking

*Using bugs, glitches, or third-party software to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

  • Report bugs: If you discover an exploit or bug, report it to the staff immediately.
  • No external mods: Hacking or using unauthorized modifications will result in an immediate ban.

8. Respect Law Enforcement & EMS

*Law enforcement and EMS play important roles in the server, and their authority must be respected.

  • No cop-baiting: Don’t provoke law enforcement to create chaotic or unrealistic situations.
  • Realistic injuries: If injured, roleplay the effects and engage EMS in the scene realistically.

9. New Life Rule (NLR)

*After death, your character must forget everything leading to their demise.

  • Forget previous events: You cannot seek revenge or return to the scene after dying.
  • Return restrictions: Wait at least 15 minutes before returning to the area and avoid rejoining the same scene.

10. Value Your Life (VLR)

*Your character’s life must be valued in dangerous situations.

  • Fear RP: Show fear when appropriate, such as when threatened with a weapon.
  • Avoid recklessness: Don’t engage in suicidally reckless behavior unless it’s justified by your character’s development.
